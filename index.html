About Empresario

Empresario is the annual entrepreneurial summit organized by the Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur. It serves as a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to showcase their innovative ideas and business plans.

With over 50,000 participants from across the country, Empresario has established itself as one of India's premier startup events.
